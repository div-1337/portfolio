The Voice Behind

I am Manish Chaudhary — a freelance voiceover artist, multilingual AI trainer, and audio quality specialist based in India, with over three years of professional experience at the intersection of voice performance and artificial intelligence.

My voice work spans Hindi, Hinglish, and Indian English, with a track record of delivering studio-quality recordings for global AI companies including ElevenLabs, Babel Audio, Alignerr, DIP Audio, and Agents Only Technologies. My recordings power speech recognition systems, text-to-speech engines, and conversational AI models.

Beyond the microphone, I bring deep expertise in audio quality assurance — evaluating datasets against precise technical parameters including RMS loudness, clipping thresholds, DC offset, and sample rate consistency. I understand both the performance and the engineering side of voice data.

Whether you need a voice talent who can deliver natural, culturally authentic speech, a QA specialist who can audit an entire audio dataset, or an AI trainer who speaks the language of both linguistics and machine learning — I am built for that work.
